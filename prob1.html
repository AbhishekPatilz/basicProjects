1. What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI-powered language model created by OpenAI that can understand and generate human-like text. You can chat with it, ask questions, and use it for learning, coding, writing, and much more.

2. Is ChatGPT free to use?

Yes! ChatGPT offers a free version with core features. A paid plan (ChatGPT Plus) provides faster responses, priority access, and newer models like GPT-4.

3. Do I need to install ChatGPT?

No installation required. You can use it directly in your browser at chat.openai.com or via the mobile app on iOS and Android.

4. Can ChatGPT write code?

Yes! ChatGPT can generate, debug, and explain code in many programming languages including C, Java, Python, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

5. Can ChatGPT make mistakes?

Yes. While ChatGPT is powerful, it may sometimes give incorrect or outdated information. Always verify important details from trusted sources.

6. Is my data safe with ChatGPT?

Conversations may be used to improve the model. Avoid sharing personal, sensitive, or confidential information while chatting.

7. What are some cool things I can try with ChatGPT?

You can use ChatGPT for learning new skills, writing stories, creating resumes, practicing interviews, solving coding problems, and even fun activities like jokes, riddles, and travel planning.

8. How is ChatGPT different from Google Search?

Google Search shows links to information, while ChatGPT directly generates answers in natural language. Both are useful, but ChatGPT feels more like a personal assistant.

9. Can I use ChatGPT offline?

No, ChatGPT requires an internet connection because it runs on powerful servers hosted by OpenAI.

10. How do I get better responses from ChatGPT?

Ask clear and specific questions. The more details you give, the better ChatGPT can understand and respond.
